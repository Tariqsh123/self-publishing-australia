Privacy Policy

Effective from: 1st May 2026

Self Publishing Australia ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, hold, use, and disclose your personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

We are committed to safeguarding the personal information of our authors, website visitors, and clients across Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth, Adelaide, and all other Australian regions.

1. What Personal Information We Collect

The types of personal information we collect depend on your interaction with us. This may include:

Category Examples
Identity Information Full name, pen name, date of birth, author biography
Contact Information Email, phone number, residential or postal address (e.g., NSW, VIC, QLD, WA, SA)
Payment Information Bank account details, credit card info (processed securely), PayPal email, billing address
Manuscript & Content Data Your manuscript, cover images, author photo, book description, keywords, categories
KDP Account Information Amazon KDP account email (not your password)
Technical Information IP address, browser type, device info, website usage stats via cookies
Communication Records Emails, call notes, chat transcripts, support tickets

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18 years without parental consent.

2. How We Collect Your Personal Information

Method Description
Directly from you Forms, quotes, orders, newsletter signups, calls/emails
Through your manuscript Author bio, acknowledgements, dedications
Automatically via website Cookies, analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics)
From third parties (with consent) PayPal, Stripe, bank confirmations, Amazon KDP if authorized

We collect information lawfully and fairly, never through deceptive or intrusive methods.

3. Why We Collect, Hold, and Use Your Personal Information

Primary purposes and APP basis:

Purpose Legal Basis (APP)
Provide publishing services (formatting, cover design, KDP setup) APP 3 – Necessary for service delivery
Communicate project status, revisions, approvals APP 3 – Directly related to services
Process payments and issue invoices APP 3 – Necessary for billing
Verify identity and manuscript ownership APP 3 – Risk management & fraud prevention
Improve website & services via analytics APP 3 – Legitimate business interests
Send marketing communications (opt-in only) APP 7 – Direct marketing with consent
Comply with legal obligations (e.g., tax) APP 3 – Compliance with law

4. Disclosure to Third Parties & Overseas Transfer

We only share personal information when necessary to deliver services or comply with law. Key recipients include:

Recipient Purpose Safeguards
Amazon KDP Book upload & publication Only necessary metadata; you retain full account control
Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal, bank) Payments & refunds PCI-DSS compliant; we do not store card details
Email marketing platform (Mailchimp/Klaviyo) Newsletters & updates GDPR/APP compliant; unsubscribe managed
Cloud storage (Google Drive, Dropbox) Share manuscript proofs & cover drafts securely Password-protected / expiring links
Legal authorities Comply with court orders / law Only legally required disclosure
Professional advisors Tax compliance, dispute resolution, legal advice Confidentiality obligations

Some service providers may be overseas (USA, Ireland, Singapore, Luxembourg). We ensure they comply with APP or equivalent laws. Manuscripts are not sent overseas without consent unless required for service delivery.

5. Data Security & Breach Response

We implement strong security measures including SSL/TLS encryption, access controls, password protection, 2FA, regular audits, and a Data Breach Response Plan compliant with APP 11. In case of a serious breach, we notify affected individuals and the OAIC within 30 days.

6. Data Retention

Type of Information Retention Period
Manuscript & final published files 7 years from project completion
Invoices & payment records 7 years (ATO compliance)
Marketing contact lists Until unsubscribe or deletion request
Website analytics data 26 months (Google Analytics default)

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete personal information, and make complaints. Contact us at [Insert Email] / [Insert Phone Number].

8. Cookies & Website Analytics

Our website uses essential, analytics, and marketing cookies (with consent). You can control cookies via browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. If we inadvertently collect info from a child, we delete it promptly. Parents/guardians may contact us regarding their child's data.

10. Anonymity & Pseudonyms

You may use a pen name for general inquiries, but legal name is required for invoicing, copyright verification, and payment processing.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or website notice at least 14 days before taking effect.

12. Complaints & Dispute Resolution

Internal complaints can be submitted via email. Investigation completed within 30 days. If unresolved, you may escalate to the OAIC within 90 days.
