The Rescue

Investment From $2,990 AUD

Sometimes things go wrong. This package is for authors who are having trouble with their book when it is already in production.

Maybe your editor has fallen ill, maybe the company doing your book has closed down, or maybe you’re just not that happy with how it’s all going.

Don’t worry; we can help you finish your book, whether you need editing, layout or even just getting your book wrapped up to send to the printer. The package will be customised to your particular rescue needs, and we will work closely with you to get your book finished.

If you’d like to find out more about this package or you’d like to book in, book a free, no-obligation 30-minute Discovery Call with a member of the Publish Central team.

Please Note

  • The final cost of your package will depend on the length of your book and the level of work required.
  • We use only the best service providers. Everybody on our team has experience in or with a major publishing company, including Penguin, John Wiley & Sons, Random House, Lonely Planet, Oxford University Press, RMIT Publishing, Hardie Grant and many more.
